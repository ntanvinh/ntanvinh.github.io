VAudio News

Your AI voice for daily news

VAudio News helps you catch up on important headlines quickly with a clean, distraction-free reading and listening experience. The app turns articles into natural speech, supports AI-powered summaries, and lets you personalize sources by language and category. It is built for busy users who want to stay informed while driving, commuting, or multitasking.

AvailableiOSAndroid

Features

Listen to News Naturally

Convert long articles into clear, natural speech so you can stay updated without staring at the screen. Adjust speed, pitch, and voice to match your listening preference.

AI Summary, Faster Catch-up

Get concise AI-generated summaries that highlight key points before you dive into full content. This helps you prioritize what matters and save time every day.

Smart Source Personalization

Filter and manage news sources by language and category to build a feed that fits your interests. Keep only relevant content and reduce information noise.

Built for Driving Moments

Use car-focused controls with larger buttons, higher contrast, and audio-friendly interaction for safer, easier use on the move.
